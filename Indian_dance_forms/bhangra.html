About Bhangra:

Bhangra is a lively and energetic dance form originating from Punjab, India. It is traditionally performed during the harvest festival, Baisakhi, but has become popular worldwide. The dance is characterized by vigorous movements, lively rhythms, and a vibrant, colorful style that represents the celebratory spirit of the Punjabi people.
